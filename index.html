Phumelela is a non-profit organization that aims to provide educational resources, life skills, and recreational support to underprivileged children with

Food Assistance

Providing nutritious meals and food stamps to ensure children have access to proper nutrition and health

Educational Support

Facilitating access to quality education, school supplies, and tutoring to enhance learning outcomes

Life Skills & Recreational

Offering workshops and activities to develop essential life skills and promote physical and mental well-being

What we stand for

Our Mission

To empower disadvantaged children through comprehensive support, enabling them to reach their full potential.

Our Vision

A society where all children have access to the resources and opportunities they need to thrive

Our Values

Compassion, Empowerment, integrity and sustainability
